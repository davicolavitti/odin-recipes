Lasagna Recipes

Description

This delicious lasagna can be prepared quickly while preserving its incredible and delicious flavor! in just 15 minutes.

we will see how we will make this tasty dish for your family or friends.

Lasagna Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Cheeses
  • Lasagna noodle
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna acording to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
